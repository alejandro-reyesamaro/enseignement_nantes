{\bf{{\large Exercice 2:}} } Prouvez que \ded{$\Gamma$}{$\Delta$}, où :
\begin{align*}
\Gamma &= \left\{\exists x\, \left(p(x) \land \forall y\, \left(d(y) \imp l(x,y)\right)\right),\forall x\, \left(p(x) \imp \forall y\,\left(g(y)\imp \lnot l(x,y)\right)\right)\right\}\\
\Delta &= \forall x\, \left(d(x)\imp \lnot g(x)\right)
\end{align*}
